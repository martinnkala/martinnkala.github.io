Temples

While we hope to someday service Temple patrons at every Temple location, we currently only service four areas. Please see the visit the pages below for more details on each Temple.

◀ ▶
“And they pitched their tents round about the temple, every man having his tent with the door thereof towards the temple...” - Mosiah 2:6

Salt Lake Utah Temple

The Salt Lake Temple is the largest Temple of the Church, and the first to feature a standing angel Moroni statue, which is 14-feet tall. Rich symbolism adorns the exterior of the Salt Lake Temple, depicting mankind's journey from mortality into the eternal realms.

Jordan River Utah Temple

The Jordan River Temple is the namesake of the Jordan River, which flows about a mile east of the temple on its course through the valley. At the entrance to the temple, visitors are greeted by a striking water fountain, which spouts among vividly colored flowers and shrubs.

Draper Utah Temple

Standing high on a ridgeline near the mouth of Draper's Corner Canyon, the Draper Utah Temple offers a inspiring view of the entire Salt Lake Valley.

Tooele Valley Utah Temple

The Tooele Valley Temple was announced in the Sunday afternoon session of General Conference, April 2019, by President Russel M Nelson. Details forthcoming.
